Kruti Shah

Kruti is an Architect who likes to explore the intersection between Art, Architecture and technology. Light, spaces and interactive art are the mediums she prefers to work with in her practice. She’s more of a fabricator who likes to create, experiment and destroy things. She also tries to inculcate society, culture and nature into her art, questioning reality like in one of her projects ‘Hunger’, which talks about the hunger crisis in the 21st century. Technology in art gives her more freedom to explore the spaces which are more fluid in nature, responding to the environment that surrounds it. She aims to experiment with more site specific interactive installation, as every space has a story to tell.
